ABOUT

Gather at The Pond to cultivate experiences and forge lasting connections with people you actually want to meet. The Pond is an app for those who don't just want to meet people with certain interests, but want to meet people with certain credentials as well. Using only fully-verified information, users can create groups and events with very specific parameters to ensure high-value connections.

Want to create a community of board-certified doctors who went to your university and live in your city?
The Pond can verify education and professional certifications.

Looking to put together an angel investing club?
The Pond allows you to filter potential members by verified income and assets.

Want to expand your coding knowledge with a group of similarly skilled engineers?
The Pond can ensure that everyone at your next tech talk has a minimum familiarity with development.

See how leveraging your information can open up new opportunities in your life!

YOUR DATA

Self-reported data is far too easy to exaggerate, inflate, or even fabricate. At The Pond, all user information is fully verified through third-party sources, so there isn't even a question of authenticity. This also allows for much more selective filtering when creating and searching for groups and events to ensure high-value connections that work for all parties. Verifiable data points include:

Intelligence (IQ)*

Income*

Spending Habits*

Net Worth*

Assets*

Credit Score*

Professional Certs

Military Service

Height

Employer

School(s) Attended

Location

Travel History

Airline Status

Hotel Status

*For your safety and security, the starred data points will never be revealed to other users - they will simply be used for filtering purposes. Additionally, this information will never leave your phone un-encrypted, so you don't have to worry about your information getting lost or stolen. With our fully customizable privacy settings, all data points will be shown at your discretion!
