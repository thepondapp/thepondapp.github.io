Frequently Asked Questions


General FAQ


How does The Pond keep my data secure?

We use strong security measures to ensure that only you can access your info. This involves measures such as Multi-Factor Authentication, data encryption in transition and at rest, and identity verification to make sure your data is never compromised.

Is The Pond free to use?

The Pond is free and always will be! We plan to add optional premium features in the future to enhance your experience.

How can I get better matches?

There are three things you can do to get better matches:

  1. Be as specific as possible about who you're looking for in a co-founder or early-stage team member.
  2. Fill out your profile in as much detail as possible.
  3. Use the app regularly! This will put your profile in front of more potential connections.
